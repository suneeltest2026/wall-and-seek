How to Play

1. Roles

The host picks how many Seekers there are. Everyone else is a Hider.

2. Hiding

Hiders get a short window to move around the room and find a spot before Seekers can move at all.

3. Become Wall

Each Hider gets a limited number of "Become Wall" taps. While active, you're frozen and shielded — a Seeker's catch fails against you. No cooldown between uses, but each use is limited in duration and you have a limited number of them.

4. Catching

Walk close to a Hider as a Seeker and tap Tag/Catch. If they still have call-outs in reserve, they wriggle free instead of being eliminated.

5. Turning the Tables

As a Hider, walk close to a Seeker and tap Call Out to instantly swap roles with them.

6. Winning

Last player active wins, or Seekers win by catching every Hider, or Hiders win if time runs out with any still free.
